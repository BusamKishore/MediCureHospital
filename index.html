Our Story

Trusted Healthcare Since 1999

MediCurePlus Hospital was founded with a single vision: to bring world-class medical care to every patient, regardless of their background. Over 25 years, we've grown into one of India's most trusted multi-specialty hospitals, combining advanced technology with genuine human compassion.

Accredited by NABH & JCI, our 800-bed facility in the heart of Andhra Pradesh serves over 2 lakh patients annually, with centres of excellence in Cardiology, Oncology, Neurology, and more.

Our Services

Comprehensive Medical Care

From preventive care to complex surgeries, we offer a complete spectrum of healthcare services under one roof.

🫀

Cardiology & Cardiac Surgery

Full-spectrum cardiac care including angioplasty, bypass surgery, valve replacement, and cardiac catheterization. Our CATH lab operates 24/7 for heart emergencies.

NABH Certified Centre
🧠

Neurology & Neurosurgery

Expert treatment for brain tumors, epilepsy, stroke, Parkinson's disease, spinal disorders, and complex neurological conditions using robotic-assisted precision surgery.

3T MRI Available
🦴

Orthopaedics & Joint Care

Joint replacement (knee, hip, shoulder), arthroscopy, spine surgery, fracture care, sports injuries, and advanced physiotherapy & rehabilitation programs.

Robotic Surgery
🎗️

Oncology & Cancer Care

Comprehensive cancer treatment including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, immunotherapy, targeted therapy, and precision oncology. Bone marrow transplant unit available.

Linac Radiotherapy
👶

Paediatrics & Neonatology

Specialized children's healthcare from birth through adolescence. Level III NICU for premature and critically ill newborns, managed by experienced neonatologists.

Level III NICU
🫁

Pulmonology & Chest Care

Treatment of asthma, COPD, interstitial lung disease, sleep apnea, tuberculosis, and lung transplant evaluation. Modern bronchoscopy and spirometry suites.

Sleep Lab Available
👁️

Ophthalmology

LASIK, cataract surgery, retina detachment repair, corneal transplant, glaucoma treatment, and pediatric eye care. State-of-the-art femtosecond laser facility.

Laser Vision Centre
🦷

Dental & Maxillofacial

Dental implants, orthodontics, oral surgery, TMJ disorders, reconstructive jaw surgery, and cosmetic dentistry. Digital smile design and 3D dental imaging.

3D Dental Imaging
🧪

Diagnostics & Pathology

Advanced imaging (1.5T & 3T MRI, 128-slice CT, PET-CT), digital X-ray, 4D ultrasound, NABL-accredited lab with same-day reports and home collection.

NABL Accredited Lab
